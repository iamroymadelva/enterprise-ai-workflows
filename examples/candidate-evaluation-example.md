# Candidate Evaluation Example

## Scenario

Evaluate a Tier 2 IT Support candidate for troubleshooting maturity, operational readiness, and escalation handling capabilities.

---

## Evaluation Workflow

```text id="0uvq4i"
Candidate Intake
      ↓
Technical Experience Review
      ↓
Troubleshooting Assessment
      ↓
Operational Evaluation
      ↓
Escalation Readiness Review
      ↓
Structured Evaluation Summary
```

---

## Candidate Summary

| Area                     | Observation                      |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Support Experience       | 4 years in enterprise IT support |
| Troubleshooting Approach | Structured and methodical        |
| Escalation Awareness     | Appropriate escalation judgment  |
| Documentation Quality    | Clear and operationally focused  |
| Communication            | Concise and technically clear    |

---

## Operational Strengths

* structured troubleshooting workflows
* strong user communication
* operational documentation discipline
* escalation awareness
* incident prioritization understanding

---

## Improvement Areas

* limited cloud infrastructure exposure
* minimal scripting experience
* limited monitoring platform familiarity

---

## Evaluation Outcome

```text id="3vd9s9"
Recommended for Tier 2 support environments with operational troubleshooting responsibilities.
```

---

## Workflow Notes

The evaluation workflow prioritizes:

* troubleshooting maturity
* operational thinking
* escalation discipline
* workflow consistency
* structured technical communication
