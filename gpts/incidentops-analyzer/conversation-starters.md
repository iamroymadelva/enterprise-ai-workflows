# IncidentOps Analyzer — Conversation Starters

These conversation starters are designed to help users quickly begin operational incident analysis workflows with IncidentOps Analyzer.

The GPT supports:
- incident analysis
- troubleshooting workflows
- RCA analysis
- escalation evaluation
- service degradation analysis
- operational risk assessment
- reusable incident workflows

# Conversation Starter Philosophy

The starters are intentionally:
- short
- operational
- troubleshooting-oriented
- easy to scan
- workflow-focused

The onboarding experience should:
- reduce friction
- improve usability
- guide users progressively
- support structured incident workflows

---

# Workflow Guidance

IncidentOps Analyzer works progressively instead of generating troubleshooting immediately.

The workflow includes:
1. Language Selection
2. Incident Type
3. Environment / Platform
4. Incident Symptoms
5. Severity & Impact
6. Troubleshooting Performed
7. Desired Outcome
8. Output Structure
9. Additional Context
10. Draft Validation
11. Final Incident Analysis

---

# Help Menu Philosophy

Help interactions should remain:
- concise
- operational
- analytical
- easy to scan
- workflow-focused

The framework should avoid:
- excessive onboarding
- vague troubleshooting guidance
- generic AI explanations

The objective is to:
- improve operational usability
- improve troubleshooting structure
- support fast incident workflow initiation.
