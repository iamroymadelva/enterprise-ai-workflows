# Candidate Evaluator Workflow

This document describes the operational workflow lifecycle, staged interaction model, and hiring analysis orchestration patterns used by Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator.

The workflow is designed to support structured hiring analysis, operational readiness evaluation, and support-role candidate assessment through progressive operational interactions.

---

# Workflow Philosophy

Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator treats hiring analysis as a structured operational workflow instead of generic resume scoring.

The workflow prioritizes:

* operational survivability
* troubleshooting maturity
* customer-facing readiness
* hiring risk visibility
* ATS-aware evaluation
* structured comparison workflows

The system is designed to progressively improve hiring realism and evaluation consistency throughout the workflow lifecycle.

---

# Shared Workflow Lifecycle

Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator follows a staged hiring workflow model.

```text id="jlwmmq"
Language Selection
      ↓
Workflow Selection
      ↓
Job Description Analysis
      ↓
Role Detection
      ↓
Resume Evaluation
      ↓
Operational Readiness Assessment
      ↓

      ↓

```

This structure improves:

* hiring consistency
* recruiter readability
* operational fit analysis
* structured candidate comparison
* support-role alignment

---

# Workflow Stages

## 1. Language Selection

The workflow begins by identifying the preferred analysis language.

Supported languages:

* English
* Español

The selected language remains consistent throughout the evaluation workflow unless explicitly changed.

---

## 2. Workflow Selection

The workflow identifies the hiring analysis objective before candidate evaluation begins.

Supported workflows include:

* single candidate analysis
* multi-candidate comparison
* ATS alignment review
* operational readiness analysis
* troubleshooting maturity evaluation
* hiring risk detection
* interview question generation
* resume optimization

The workflow intentionally avoids isolated resume scoring without workflow context.

---

## 3. Job Description Analysis

The workflow uses the Job Description as the primary operational evaluation context.

```text id="jlwmmp"
Job Description
      ↓
Role Focus Detection
      ↓
Operational Priorities
      ↓
Evaluation Alignment
```

This improves:

* role alignment
* hiring realism
* operational evaluation consistency
* ATS relevance

If no Job Description is provided, the workflow defaults to enterprise IT Support and Service Desk standards.

---

## 4. Role Detection

The workflow automatically identifies the operational role focus.

Supported role categories include:

* IT Support
* Service Desk
* Technical Support
* SaaS Support
* Desktop Support
* Customer Support Engineering
* NOC Support

Role detection influences:

* evaluation priorities
* operational expectations
* troubleshooting depth
* hiring recommendation logic

---

## 5. Resume Evaluation

The workflow analyzes candidate resumes using operational support hiring criteria.

## Evaluation Areas

* troubleshooting experience
* support tooling exposure
* customer-facing capability
* escalation maturity
* operational ownership
* support environment exposure

The workflow intentionally avoids overvaluing buzzwords or certifications without practical evidence.

---

## 6. Operational Readiness Assessment

Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator evaluates whether candidates are likely to succeed in real support operations environments.

```text id="jlwmmo"
Technical Exposure
      ↓
Troubleshooting Evidence
      ↓
Operational Ownership
      ↓
Support Readiness
      ↓
Hiring Viability
```

This improves:

* hiring realism

* support survivability analysis
* candidate differentiation

---

## 7. ATS Alignment Review

The workflow supports ATS-aware evaluation without allowing keyword density to dominate hiring decisions.

## ATS Evaluation Areas

* role-relevant terminology
* support tooling alignment
* operational relevance
* realistic technical exposure
* resume structure quality

The workflow intentionally avoids:

* keyword stuffing prioritization
* inflated ATS scoring
* unrealistic optimization strategies

---

## 8. Hiring Risk Analysis

The workflow identifies operational and behavioral hiring risks.

## Risk Areas

* vague responsibilities
* unsupported technical claims
* weak troubleshooting evidence
* inconsistent experience
* limited customer-facing exposure
* generic resume language

This improves structured hiring decision support.

---

## 9. Structured Hiring Recommendation

Outputs are optimized for:

* recruiter readability
* concise operational summaries
* markdown scanning
* structured scoring
* hiring recommendation clarity
* operational risk visibility
