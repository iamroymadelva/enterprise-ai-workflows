# Candidate Evaluator Architecture

This document describes the operational architecture, workflow structure, and hiring evaluation patterns used by Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator.

The system is designed as a workflow-oriented hiring analysis module within the Enterprise AI Workflows ecosystem.

---

# Operational Purpose

Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator is built to support structured hiring analysis for enterprise support environments instead of generic resume reviews.

The architecture prioritizes:

* operational readiness
* troubleshooting maturity
* hiring risk visibility
* support workflow alignment
* structured candidate evaluation
* ATS-aware operational analysis

The system is designed to behave like an operational hiring workflow engine rather than a generic recruiting assistant.

Enterprise IT Candidate Evaluator uses progressive operational evaluation workflows instead of isolated resume scoring.

## Workflow Layers

| Layer            | Operational Function                     |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Intake Layer     | Workflow and role selection              |
| Role Layer       | Job description and role focus detection |

# Hiring Evaluation Model

The workflow is designed around operational support hiring analysis.

## Workflow Priorities

* evaluate operational survivability
* prioritize troubleshooting maturity
* validate customer-facing capability
* identify escalation readiness
* detect hiring risks
* reduce ATS overreliance

The workflow intentionally prioritizes operational realism over keyword density.

# Operational Readiness Architecture

The workflow evaluates whether candidates are likely to succeed in real support operations environments.

## Evaluation Areas

* troubleshooting ownership
* ticket queue exposure
* escalation handling
* customer-facing communication
* multitasking readiness
* enterprise environment familiarity

This improves support-role hiring realism and operational fit visibility.

# ATS Evaluation Logic

The architecture includes ATS-aware evaluation without allowing keyword matching to dominate hiring decisions.

## ATS Evaluation Areas

* role-relevant terminology
* support tooling relevance
* operational alignment
* realistic technical exposure
* resume structure quality

The workflow intentionally avoids:

* keyword stuffing prioritization
* inflated ATS scoring
* unrealistic ranking inflation

---

# Hiring Risk Architecture

The workflow evaluates operational and behavioral hiring risks.

## Risk Indicators

* vague troubleshooting experience
* generic resume wording
* unsupported technical claims
* lack of customer-facing evidence
* inconsistent career progression
* weak operational ownership

This improves structured hiring analysis and recruiter decision support.
