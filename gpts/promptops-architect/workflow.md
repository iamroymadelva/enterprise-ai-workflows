# PromptOps Workflow

This document describes the operational workflow lifecycle, staged interaction model, and AI workflow orchestration patterns used by PromptOps Architect.

The workflow is designed to support reusable AI workflow systems, structured prompt engineering, and context-aware instruction architecture through progressive operational interactions.

---

# Workflow Philosophy

PromptOps Architect treats prompt engineering as structured AI workflow architecture instead of isolated prompt writing.

The workflow prioritizes:

* instruction hierarchy
* context engineering
* workflow continuity
* ambiguity reduction
* reusable interaction systems
* iterative refinement

The system is designed to progressively improve AI interaction quality throughout the workflow lifecycle.

---

PromptOps Architect follows a staged AI workflow architecture model.


# Workflow Stages

## 1. Language Selection

The workflow begins by identifying the preferred session language.

Supported languages:

* English
* Español

The selected language remains consistent throughout the workflow lifecycle unless explicitly changed.

---

## 2. AI Platform Selection

The workflow identifies the target AI platform before prompt construction begins.

Supported platforms include:

* ChatGPT
* Claude
* Gemini
* Cursor
* Copilot
* Midjourney
* custom AI systems

The workflow intentionally avoids assuming identical AI behavior across platforms.

---

## 3. Goal Definition

The workflow identifies the primary AI objective.

Examples include:

* analyze
* optimize
* create
* automate
* summarize
* explain

This stage establishes the strategic direction of the workflow.

## 4. Role Definition
